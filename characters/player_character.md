## Combat Stats
*   **Armor Class (AC):** 17 (15 without Shield)
*   **Initiative:** +1
*   **Speed:** 25 ft.
*   **Hit Points (HP):** 10
*   **Hit Dice:** 1d8

## Proficiencies & Languages
*   **Armor:** Light, Medium, Shields
*   **Weapons:** All simple weapons
*   **Tools:** Tinker's Tools (Dwarven Tool Proficiency), Herbalism Kit (Hermit Background).
*   **Saving Throws:** Wisdom, Charisma
*   **Skills:** Insight (+5), Medicine (+5), Arcana (+4, Double Proficiency), Religion (+4, Double Proficiency), History (+2), Persuasion (+1).
*   **Languages:** Common, Dwarvish, Undercommon, Deep Speech, Abyssal.

## Features & Traits
*   **Discovery:** The quiet seclusion of your hermitage gave you access to a unique and powerful discovery. The exact nature of this revelation will be revealed as the campaign progresses (it is likely related to the **Black Shard**).
*   **Spellcasting:** You can cast divine spells using your Wisdom.
*   **Blessings of Knowledge:** You learn two additional languages and gain proficiency in two of the following skills: Arcana, History, Nature, or Religion. Your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make that uses either of those skills.
*   **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
*   **Dwarven Resilience:** Advantage on saving throws against poison, and resistance against poison damage.
*   **Stonecunning:** Double proficiency bonus on Intelligence (History) checks related to stonework.
*   **Dwarven Armor Training:** You have proficiency with light and medium armor.

## Spells
*   **Spellcasting Ability:** Wisdom
*   **Spell Save DC:** 13 (8 + Proficiency + Wisdom mod)
*   **Spell Attack Bonus:** +5 (Proficiency + Wisdom mod)
*   **Cantrips:** Guidance, Mending, Toll the Dead.
*   **1st Level Slots:** 2
*   **Always Prepared (Domain):** *Command, Identify*.
*   **Daily Prepared Spells (4 total):** Guiding Bolt, Healing Word, Bane, Detect Magic.

## Backstory & Concept
*   **Concept:** Drawn to a power that is cold in temperature, obscure, weak and sorrowful. Interested in the long game and to incrementally build and create. Focus is on the building and crafting of both magical and nonmagical items, though avoiding the traditional 'forge' or smithing motif. Committed to building and growing own power. Solitary by nature but aware that not all can be done alone.
*   **The Reliquary:** A small, heavy lead box containing a small, dark black shard of unknown origin. It feels as if the shard vibrates at a frequency beyond hearing; the ambition is to attune to that exact, internal rhythm.
