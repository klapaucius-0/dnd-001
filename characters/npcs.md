# NPC Log

## Current Companions
*   **Kaelen:** A human guide who survived the Frozen Gap. He is currently traveling with Wael and seeking shelter in Oakhaven. He is physically weak but knows the local geography and lore of the "watchers."

## Known NPCs
*   **Mother Halsa:** An elderly hunter/hermit living at the timberline. She seems to know Kaelen and is wary of the mountain's dangers.
*   **Toren & Hov:** Guards at Oakhaven's North Gate. Weary but reasonable.
*   **Berath (Deceased):** Wael's "found family." A hermit. Murdered in Oakhaven for the Dark Black Shard. Wael found his satchel at the Cold-Iron Obelisk after killing the monster that took it.
*   **Pale-Eyed Figures:** Dangerous, frost-filled monsters that roam the Frozen Gap. They killed Berath and nearly killed Wael.
