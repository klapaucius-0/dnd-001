# Session 0: Character Creation Template
*This file contains the mandatory step-by-step logic for generating a protagonist in "The Sorrow."*

## Phase 1: Rule Alignment
1.  **Variant Rules:** AI DM provides an exhaustive list of common Variant Rules (Encumbrance, Feats, Multiclassing, etc.) from the 2014 ruleset.
2.  **Confirmation:** Wait for player to finalize rule selection.

## Phase 2: Sequential Creation
*AI DM guides the player through the following steps strictly one at a time. For every step, the AI MUST provide an exhaustive list of all available options from the allowed sourcebooks (PHB, XGE, SCAG, TCOE), including exact mechanics, proficiencies, and descriptions.*

1.  **Concept:** Discuss character theme and narrative role.
2.  **Race/Subrace:** Provide all mechanical details and lore context.
3.  **Class/Archetype:** Detail features from Level 1 to the current campaign level.
4.  **Ability Scores:** Guide through method selection (Point Buy, Array, or Roll).
5.  **Background:** Provide exhaustive lists of features and proficiencies.
6.  **Equipment:** Offer choice between Class/Background kit or Starting Wealth.

## Phase 3: Initialization
1.  **HUD Setup:** Populate `state/character_state.md`.
2.  **Log Setup:** Initialize `logs/experience_log.md` and `logs/currency_log.md`.
3.  **Narrative Anchor:** Write the character's backstory and mission into the archive or lore atlas.
