# Progression: Cleric (Forge Domain)
<!-- SOURCE: PHB, XGtE, TCoE -->

This document outlines the standard progression for a Cleric of the Forge Domain from Levels 1 to 20, incorporating core rules and optional features.

## Class Overview
*   **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per Cleric level.
*   **Armor Proficiencies:** Light, Medium, Heavy (Forge), Shields.
*   **Weapon Proficiencies:** Simple Weapons.
*   **Tool Proficiencies:** Smith's Tools (Forge).
*   **Saving Throws:** Wisdom, Charisma.
*   **Skills:** Choose two from History, Insight, Medicine, Persuasion, Religion.

## Multiclassing & Origin Customization

### Multiclassing Requirements
*   **Ability Score Minimum:** Wisdom 13.
*   **Proficiencies Gained (if joining Cleric):** Light armor, medium armor, shields.

### Customizing Your Origin (TCoE)
If using these rules, you can swap racial traits to better suit the Forge Domain:
*   **Ability Scores:** Move racial ASIs to any score (e.g., +2 STR / +2 CON for Mountain Dwarf).
*   **Languages:** Swap any racial language for another.
*   **Proficiencies:** Swap racial weapon/armor proficiencies for tool proficiencies (e.g., swapping Dwarf's Warhammer proficiency for another set of artisan's tools).

## Spellcasting Mechanics (PHB)
*   **Spell Save DC:** 8 + Proficiency Bonus + Wisdom Modifier.
*   **Spell Attack Bonus:** Proficiency Bonus + Wisdom Modifier.
*   **Prepared Spells:** Wisdom Modifier + Cleric Level (min 1).
*   **Ritual Casting:** You can cast a cleric spell as a ritual if that spell has the ritual tag and you have the spell prepared.
*   **Spellcasting Focus:** You can use a holy symbol (amulet, emblem on shield, or reliquary) as a spellcasting focus.

## Forge Domain Features (XGtE)

### Domain Spells
You always have these spells prepared. They do not count against your limit of prepared spells.
*   **1st Level:** *Identify, Searing Smite*
*   **3rd Level:** *Heat Metal, Magic Weapon*
*   **5th Level:** *Elemental Weapon, Protection from Energy*
*   **7th Level:** *Fabricate, Wall of Fire*
*   **9th Level:** *Animate Objects, Creation*

### Level 1: Bonus Proficiencies
Gain proficiency with heavy armor and smith's tools.

### Level 1: Blessing of the Forge
At the end of a long rest, touch one nonmagical object (suit of armor or a simple/martial weapon). Until your next long rest or you die, the object becomes a magic item (+1 to AC if armor, +1 to attack/damage if weapon).

### Level 2: Channel Divinity: Artisan's Blessing
Spend 1 hour (can be during a short rest) to conduct a ritual creating a nonmagical metal item (worth <= 100gp). You must provide metal of equal value (coins, scrap, etc.).

### Level 6: Soul of the Forge
*   Resistance to fire damage.
*   While wearing heavy armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC.

### Level 8: Divine Strike
Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 fire damage. At 14th level, this increases to 2d8.

### Level 17: Saint of Forge and Fire
*   Immunity to fire damage.
*   While wearing heavy armor, you have resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

## Optional Class Features (TCoE)

### Level 1: Additional Cleric Spells
The following spells are added to the cleric spell list for you:
*   **1st Level:** *Protection from Evil and Good*
*   **3rd Level:** *Aura of Vitality, Spirit Shroud*
*   **4th Level:** *Aura of Life, Aura of Purity*
*   **5th Level:** *Summon Celestial*
*   **6th Level:** *Sunbeam*
*   **9th Level:** *Power Word Heal, Sunburst*

### Level 2: Harness Divine Power
As a bonus action, expend a use of Channel Divinity to regain one expended spell slot (level <= half proficiency bonus, rounded up).
*   **Uses:** 1 at 2nd level, 2 at 6th level, 3 at 18th level.

### Level 4: Cantrip Versatility
Whenever you gain an ASI level, you can replace one cantrip you know with another from the cleric list.

### Level 8: Blessed Strikes (Replaces Divine Strike)
Optional: Replace Divine Strike with this feature. Once on each of your turns when you deal damage with a cantrip or weapon attack, deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage.

## Core Cleric Features (PHB)

### Spellcasting
*   **Casting Ability:** Wisdom.
*   **Ritual Casting:** You can cast cleric spells as rituals if you have them prepared.
*   **Holy Symbol:** Used as a spellcasting focus.

### Channel Divinity
*   **Turn Undead:** As an action, each undead within 30ft that can see/hear you must make a Wis save or be turned for 1 minute or until it takes damage.

### Destroy Undead
When an undead fails its save against Turn Undead, it is instantly destroyed if its CR is at or below the threshold shown in the table.

### Divine Intervention (Level 10)
As an action, request aid from your deity. Roll d100; if the result is <= your cleric level, the deity intervenes. If successful, you cannot use this feature again for 7 days. At Level 20, it succeeds automatically.
