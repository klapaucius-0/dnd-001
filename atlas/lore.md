# Clues & Rumors
<!-- SOURCE: lore -->

This file contains the consolidated repository of knowledge discovered during the campaign.

## The Dark Black Shard [STRATUM: 5_RESONANT_VOID]
**Discovery Date:** Day 1 / 16:00
**Source:** Found in the Yard (Wael)
**Detail:** A jagged, resonant shard found in the "Silent Yard." It pulses with a rhythmic frequency that affects nearby matter.

---

## The Reaching [STRATUM: 3_FROZEN_EXPANSE]
**Discovery Date:** Day 28 / 10:00
**Source:** Varin (Trail-Warden)
**Context:** Observed in the Western Reach.
**Detail:** A phenomenon where the mountain seems to "reach" for living things, characterized by localized gravity increases and the biological "stopping" of creatures.

---

## Lead-Lined Cargo Boxes [STRATUM: 1_FRAGILE_SURFACE]
**Discovery Date:** Day 32 / 14:00
**Source:** Alen (Carrier) / Skaen (Identify)
**Context:** Recovered from the Granite Squeeze.
**Detail:** Standard industrial luggage used by Oakhaven couriers. These are heavy-duty, lead-lined iron boxes with silver-wire gaskets, designed to be airtight and protect cargo from the mountain environment. Non-magical.

---

## The Oakhaven Tension [STRATUM: 1_FRAGILE_SURFACE]
**Discovery Date:** Day 35 / 12:46
**Source:** Varin (Trail-Warden)
**Context:** Observed upon arrival at the Boar and Barrel.
**Detail:** A palpable social friction characterized as a "different kind of silence." Unlike the biological "stopping" of the mountains, this is a heavy, watchful atmosphere of paranoia and localized scarcity.
