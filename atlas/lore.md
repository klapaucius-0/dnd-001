# Clues & Rumors

## Mysteries & Secrets
*   **The Dark Black Shard [STRATUM: 5_RESONANT_VOID]:** This is the dark shard of unknown material Wael carries. Berath was murdered for it. It is exceptionally dense and seems to act as a anchor for Void-Touched entities. It represents an unnatural gap in reality.
*   **The Damp Blue-Green Stone Box [STRATUM: 4_SUNKEN_STONE]:** A relic of the unyielding primordial rock, rounded by aeons of subterranean aquatic erosion. It acts as a dampener for the Shard's resonance.
*   **Berath's Journal (The Silence) - [CORRECTED]:** The journal is largely empty of plans or ambitions. Berath died before he could set any goals or begin any work in Oakhaven. Any previous mentions of a "Great Ruse" or "Deep Frost Network" were either misinterpretations or the projections of a man lost to the eroding reality of the mountains.
*   **The Cold-Iron Obelisk [STRATUM: 5_RESONANT_VOID]:** A weathered ruin in the Gap. Its dark metal surface responds to the resonance of the Shard, creating distortions in gravity and sound.
*   **The Mountain's Throat [STRATUM: 2_ERODING_AGE]:** A fissure at the Weeping Shelf leading to lower caverns. It is a place where the history of the world is physically collapsing into dust and masonry.
*   **The Still-Eyes (Network) - [CONCLUDED]:** Initial fears of a vast network were likely the result of mountain-madness or the shard's own influence. The Resonant Figures are solitary monsters drawn to the void.
*   **The Hum - [DEBUNKED]:** The "frequency" Kaelen spoke of was likely a hallucination brought on by extreme stress and the sensory deprivation of the higher slopes.

## Known Leads
*   **Oakhaven [STRATUM: 1_FRAGILE_SURFACE]:** Berath was murdered at the Boar and Barrel three nights ago. A local witness described a figure in a black cloak who moved with unnatural, gravity-defying speed and "wrongness." The killer used a dark blade to stab Berath after a brief, silent struggle. Berath's body was taken to the **Old Mason's Yard**.
