# Crafting Blueprint Registry (SSOT)
<!-- SOURCE: crafting -->

This file is the Single Source of Truth for all crafting costs and durations. All calculations follow official *Xanathar's Guide to Everything* (XGtE) formulas.

---

## Warhammer [BLUEPRINT] [TYPE: WEAPON] [[PHB]]
A traditional dwarven-designed warhammer.

### Crafting Requirements (XGtE)
*   **Market Value (GP):** 15 gp
*   **Crafting Time (Days):** 1.5 Days (10 gp/day)
*   **Material Cost (GP):** 7.5 gp
*   **Required Tools:** Smith's Tools

### Final Item Result
*   **Mechanical:** 1d8 [1d10] Bludgeoning. (PHB p. 149).

---

## Healer's Kit (Poultice) [BLUEPRINT] [TYPE: CONSUMABLE] [[PHB]]
A collection of bandages, salves, and splints.

### Crafting Requirements (XGtE)
*   **Market Value (GP):** 5 gp
*   **Crafting Time (Days):** 0.5 Days (10 gp/day)
*   **Material Cost (GP):** 2.5 gp
*   **Required Tools:** Herbalism Kit

### Final Item Result
*   **Mechanical:** 10 uses. As an action, you can expend one use of the kit to stabilize a creature that has 0 hit points, without needing to make a Wisdom (Medicine) check. (PHB p. 151).

## Antitoxin (Wash) [BLUEPRINT] [TYPE: CONSUMABLE] [[PHB]]
A liquid that neutralizes toxins and environmental hazards.

### Crafting Requirements (XGtE)
*   **Market Value (GP):** 50 gp
*   **Crafting Time (Days):** 5 Days (10 gp/day)
*   **Material Cost (GP):** 25 gp
*   **Required Tools:** Herbalism Kit

### Final Item Result
*   **Mechanical:** A creature that drinks this vial of liquid gains advantage on saving throws against poison for 1 hour. It confers no benefit to undead or constructs. (PHB p. 150).

## Potion of Healing [BLUEPRINT] [TYPE: CONSUMABLE] [[PHB]]
A restorative red liquid.

### Crafting Requirements (XGtE)
*   **Market Value (GP):** 50 gp
*   **Crafting Time (Days):** 1 Day (Potions/Consumables are halved)
*   **Material Cost (GP):** 25 gp
*   **Required Tools:** Herbalism Kit

### Final Item Result
*   **Mechanical:** Restore 2d4+2 HP. (PHB p. 153).
