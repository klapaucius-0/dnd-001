# Deep-Iron [MAT: DEEP_IRON] [FREQ: RARE] [HOMEBREW]
Rare geological formations from the deepest strata (e.g., North Cut). Extremely dense.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 18 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC for Artisan Checks)
*   **Mechanical Effects:** Required for Tier 3 tools and high-pressure structural frames.
*   **Refining Requirements:** Must be cold-formed. Exposure to heat destroys its molecular stability.

---

# Cold-Formed Steel [MAT: COLD_FORMED_STEEL] [FREQ: VERY_RARE] [HOMEBREW]
**CRITICAL:** This is not a raw material, but the *resulting property* of applying the Cold-Forming process to Standard Steel.

## Mechanical Utility ([HOMEBREW])
*   **Weapon:** Bypasses non-magical bludgeoning/piercing/slashing resistance for Fey and Fiends. Grants **Siege** (double damage to objects/structures).
*   **Armor:** Grant the wearer **Advantage** on Strength saving throws against being moved or knocked prone.

# Daleland Ox-Hide [MAT: DALELAND_HIDE] [FREQ: RARE] [HOMEBREW]
Heavy-cured leather from the south. Currently in "Weathered" state in Wael's inventory.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 12 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to shape)
*   **Refining Requirements:** 2 LH of oiling/cleaning with Neatsfoot Oil to restore to Standard status.

---

# Industrial Felt [MAT: FELT] [FREQ: UNCOMMON] [HOMEBREW]
High-density compressed wool.

## Properties
*   **Weight:** 5.0 lbs per bolt.
*   **Mechanical Effects:** Absorbs high-impact vibrations; ideal for lining heavy armor or prosthetic harnesses.

---

# Neatsfoot Oil [MAT: NEATSFOOT_OIL] [FREQ: UNCOMMON] [HOMEBREW]
A light, bone-rendered oil.

## Mechanical Utility ([HOMEBREW])
*   Reduces the WR of dry leather (like Weathered Ox-Hide) by 2 for the duration of a project.
