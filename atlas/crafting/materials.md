# Crystalline Shards [MATERIAL] [HOMEBREW]
Brittle, translucent minerals found in the higher slopes of the Sword Mountains.

## Properties
*   **Type:** Mineral / Catalyst
*   **Hardness Rating (HR):** 14 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC for Artisan Checks)
*   **Weight:** 1.0 lbs.
*   **Mechanical Effects:** Can be integrated into masonry or smithing to provide subtle chromatic shifts or temperature resistance.

# Dark Metal / Cold-Iron [MATERIAL] [HOMEBREW]
Rare geological formations. Requires absolute cold to maintain its properties.

## Properties
*   **Type:** Ore / Metal
*   **Hardness Rating (HR):** 18 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC for Artisan Checks)
*   **Mechanical Effects:** When cold-forged, it is lethal to supernatural entities (Fey, Fiends).
*   **Refining Requirements:** Must be cold-formed. Exposure to heat destroys its structural integrity.

# Frost-Vein Lichen [MATERIAL] [HOMEBREW]
Translucent, azure-pulsing lichen found in sub-zero crevices.

## Properties
*   **Type:** Herb / Catalyst
*   **Mechanical Effects:** Potent numbing properties; used for local anesthesia or pain mitigation.
*   **Preparation:** 2 LH (Tincture) or 8 LH (Complex Decoction).

# Ghost-Root [MATERIAL] [HOMEBREW]
Bitter, grey-green herb that grows in areas of absolute silence.

## Properties
*   **Type:** Herb
*   **Mechanical Effects:** Stimulates the senses; grants temporary relief from the sensory deprivation of the higher strata.
*   **Preparation:** 1 LH (Chewed/Raw) or 4 LH (Refined Paste).

# Weathered Ox-Hide [MATERIAL] [HOMEBREW]
Heavy-cured leather from the Dalelands that has been exposed to salt-air and moisture.

## Properties
*   **Type:** Hide
*   **Hardness Rating (HR):** 12 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to shape)
*   **Condition:** Stiff/Brittle core. Requires Neatsfoot Oil to restore.
*   **Refining Requirements:** 2 LH of oiling/cleaning to restore to Standard quality.

---

# Industrial Felt [MATERIAL] [HOMEBREW]
High-density compressed wool with high lanolin content.

## Properties
*   **Type:** Fabric / Padding
*   **Weight:** 5.0 lbs per bolt.
*   **Mechanical Effects:** Absorbs high-impact vibrations; ideal for lining heavy armor or prosthetic harnesses.

---

# Neatsfoot Oil [MATERIAL] [HOMEBREW]
A light, bone-rendered oil that remains liquid at low temperatures.

## Properties
*   **Type:** Catalyst / Refinement
*   **Mechanical Effects:** Penetrates deep into dry leather. Reduces the DC of leatherworking checks by 2 and prevents cracking during assembly.
