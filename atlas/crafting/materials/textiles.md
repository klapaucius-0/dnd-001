# Industrial Felt [MAT: FELT] [CAT: TEXTILE] [FREQ: UNCOMMON] [HOMEBREW]
High-density compressed wool.

## Properties
*   **Weight:** 5.0 lbs per bolt.
*   **Mechanical Effects:** Absorbs high-impact vibrations; ideal for lining heavy armor or prosthetic harnesses.

---

# Wool [MAT: WOOL] [CAT: TEXTILE] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
Fiber from sheep or other animals, spun into yarn and woven or felted.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 10 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to process/weave)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Excellent insulation; used for padding, blankets, and cold-weather gear.
*   **Market Value:** 5 sp per lb.

---

# Linen [MAT: LINEN] [CAT: TEXTILE] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A durable textile made from the fibers of the flax plant.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 11 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to process/weave)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Strong and breathable; ideal for summer clothing and lightweight bindings.
*   **Market Value:** 5 gp per lb (Fine linen).

---

# Hemp [MAT: HEMP] [CAT: TEXTILE] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A coarse, strong fiber from the cannabis plant, primarily used for rope and heavy canvas.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 11 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to process/weave)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** High tensile strength; the standard material for adventuring rope.
*   **Market Value:** 1 sp per lb (Raw fiber).

---

# Silk [MAT: SILK] [CAT: TEXTILE] [FREQ: RARE] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A fine, strong, and lustrous fiber produced by silkworms.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 14 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to process/weave)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Exceptionally strong for its weight; used for luxury goods and high-tensile specialized ropes.
*   **Market Value:** 10 gp per lb.

---

# Canvas [MAT: CANVAS] [CAT: TEXTILE] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
An extremely durable, plain-woven fabric used for sails, tents, and heavy gear.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 11 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to sew)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Structural textile for large-scale projects and high-durability bags.
*   **Market Value:** 1 sp per sq yard.

---

# Vellum [MAT: VELLUM] [CAT: TEXTILE] [FREQ: RARE] [[XGTE]] [STANDARD]
High-grade animal skin prepared as a writing surface; superior to parchment.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 13 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to scribe)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Used for permanent records, high-level spell scrolls, and illuminated manuscripts.
*   **Market Value:** 5 sp per sheet.

---

# Paper / Parchment [MAT: PAPER] [CAT: TEXTILE] [FREQ: UNCOMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
Thin material produced by processing wood pulp or animal skins (parchment) into writable sheets.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 12 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to scribe)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** The standard medium for spells, maps, and correspondence.
*   **Market Value:** 2 sp per sheet (Paper) / 1 sp per sheet (Parchment).

---

# Cotton [MAT: COTTON] [CAT: TEXTILE] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A soft, fluffy staple fiber that grows in a boll, used for spinning common yarns.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 10 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to process/weave)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** The standard material for common clothing and interactive bandages.
*   **Market Value:** 5 sp per lb.
