## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 10 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to shape)

# Clay [MAT: CLAY] [CAT: MINERAL] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A stiff, sticky fine-grained earth that can be molded when wet and is dried and baked to make pottery and bricks.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 10 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to mold)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Used for ceramics, molds for casting, and construction.
*   **Market Value:** 1 cp per lb.

---

# Glass [MAT: GLASS] [CAT: MINERAL] [FREQ: UNCOMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A hard, brittle, translucent material made by fusing sand with soda and lime.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 14 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to blow/shape)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Used for vials, lenses, and specialized laboratory equipment.
*   **Market Value:** 1 gp per lb.

---

# Granite [MAT: GRANITE] [CAT: MINERAL] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A hard, igneous rock with a visible crystalline texture. The standard for heavy construction.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 15 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to carve)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Essential for heavy masonry; provides high durability against physical impact.
*   **Market Value:** 5 cp per lb.

---

# Marble [MAT: MARBLE] [CAT: MINERAL] [FREQ: UNCOMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A metamorphic rock composed of recrystallized carbonate minerals. Valued for its beauty and polish.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 14 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to carve)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Used for fine masonry, sculptures, and high-tier architectural details.
*   **Market Value:** 2 sp per lb.

---

# Sand [MAT: SAND] [CAT: MINERAL] [FREQ: VERY_COMMON] [[XGTE]] [STANDARD]
Granular material composed of finely divided rock and mineral particles.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** N/A
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Essential for glassmaking. Used as an abrasive for cleaning/polishing rusted metal.
*   **Market Value:** 1 cp per lb.

---

# Chalk [MAT: CHALK] [CAT: MINERAL] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A soft, white, porous, sedimentary carbonate rock.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 5 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to shape)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Used for marking, mapping, and as a mild, dry abrasive.
*   **Market Value:** 1 cp per piece.

# Limestone [MAT: LIMESTONE] [CAT: MINERAL] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
A sedimentary rock composed mainly of calcium carbonate.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 11 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to carve)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Primary source for Lime (Potash) and a standard building stone for masonry.
*   **Market Value:** 2 cp per lb.
