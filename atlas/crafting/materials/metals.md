## Detailed Mechanics

### Adamantine `[MAT: ADAMANTINE]`
*   **Official Utility (DMG p. 150):** Adamantine is an ultrahard metal found in meteorites and extraordinary mineral veins. 
    *   **Armor:** While wearing adamantine armor, any critical hit against you becomes a normal hit.
    *   **Weapons (XGtE p. 78):** Melee weapons and ten pieces of ammunition made of adamantine are unusually effective when used to break objects. Whenever an adamantine weapon or piece of ammunition hits an object, the hit is a critical hit.

### Mithral `[MAT: MITHRAL]`
*   **Official Utility (DMG p. 184):** Mithral is a light, flexible metal. 
    *   **Armor:** If the armor normally imposes disadvantage on Stealth checks or has a Strength requirement, the mithral version of the armor doesn't.

### Silvered Weapons `[MAT: SILVERED]`
*   **Official Utility (PHB p. 148):** Some monsters that have resistance or immunity to nonmagical weapons are susceptible to silvered weapons. Silvering a single weapon or ten pieces of ammunition costs 100 gp. This cost represents not only the price of the silver, but the time and expertise needed to add silver to the weapon without making it less effective.
