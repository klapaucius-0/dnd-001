## Detailed Mechanics

### Magic Item Crafting Protocol (XGtE)
To create a magic item, a character needs several things:
1.  **A Formula:** A blueprint describing how to make the item.
2.  **Tool Proficiency:** Proficiency with the relevant artisan's tools.
3.  **An Exotic Material:** A specific part of a monster (e.g., the heart of a hydra, the claw of a dragon). The Challenge Rating (CR) of the monster depends on the rarity of the item being created.

### Harvesting Rules (House Rule Alignment)
*   **Check:** Wisdom (Survival) or Intelligence (Nature) against the Harvesting DC defined in `engine/rules/house_rules.md`.
*   **Outcome:** On a success, the character recovers 1 use of the exotic component. On a failure, the component is ruined.
