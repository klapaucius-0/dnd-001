# Bone [MAT: BONE] [CAT: ORGANIC] [FREQ: COMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
Rigid skeletal material salvaged from animals or other creatures.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 12 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to carve/shape)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Used for handles, fasteners, and specialized arrows or primitive tools.
*   **Market Value:** 1 cp per lb.

# Horn [MAT: HORN] [CAT: ORGANIC] [FREQ: UNCOMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
The hard, permanent outgrowth from the head of certain mammals, consisting of a keratin sheath around a bony core.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 13 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to carve/shape)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Can be softened with heat and shaped; used for bows, containers, and decoration.
*   **Market Value:** 5 sp per lb.

---

# Antler [MAT: ANTLER] [CAT: ORGANIC] [FREQ: UNCOMMON] [[PHB]] [STANDARD]
The branched, bony outgrowth from the head of deer and related animals, shed annually.

## Properties
*   **Workability Rating (WR):** 13 ([HOMEBREW] Base DC to carve/shape)
*   **Mechanical Utility:** Denser than standard bone; excellent for tool handles and durable fasteners.
*   **Market Value:** 3 sp per lb.
