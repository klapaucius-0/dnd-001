## Summary
A visceral warning marker established by the dwarf Skaen at the narrowest bottleneck of the Granite Squeeze to deter banditry.

## Atmosphere & Mood
*   **The "Vibe":** Predator paranoia, industrial finality, absolute judgment.
*   **Sensory Details:** The smell of cold copper and wet limestone; the rhythmic *creak* of a pine stake in the wind; the sight of jagged red runes on grey stone.
*   **Layered Sensory:** The site manifests the "Fragile Surface" through the brutal enforcement of order in a lawless space. The post-mortem rigidity of the sentinel suggests the mountain is already "claiming" the remains.

## Key Areas
### 1. The Bottleneck Ridge
*   **Description:** A forty-foot limestone cliff overlooking the narrowest point of the road.
*   **The Marker:** An impaled human female body set upon a pine stake, with the word **BANDIT** scrawled in blood-runes on the adjacent wall.

## Entities Present
*   **The Sentinel:** The stilled remains of a scavenger archer.

## Discovery & Lore
*   **The Law of the Forge:** A demonstration of Skaen's unyielding commitment to social and structural order.
*   **The Mountain's Memory:** Varin believes the stone itself records such acts of finality.
