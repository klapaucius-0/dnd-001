# Location: The Old Iron Road (Western Reach)

**Setting Context:** Sword Mountains (Foothills)

**Frequency:** [FREQUENCY: HIGH]
**Scale:** Narrative

## Summary
A narrow, ancient trade route of crushed slate and packed earth. It is a lonely scar through a dense, indifferent forest, stretching 30 miles toward the distant huddle of Oakhaven.

## Atmosphere & Mood
*   **The "Vibe":** Desolate isolation. The road offers no sanctuary; it is merely a path through a wilderness that feels increasingly crowded by silence and cold.
*   **Sensory Details:** The rhythmic crunch of boots on frozen slate; the smell of ancient pine needles and damp earth; the sight of leaden skies framed by skeletal branches.
*   **The Reach:** Human presence is a rare and fragile anomaly—a single chimney-wisp or an abandoned hearth—rather than a feature of the road.
