# The Graveyard of Berath

This document records the fallen souls who have walked this bleak frontier and the circumstances of their final, cold moments.

## Fallen Heroes

### Wael (Mountain Dwarf Cleric - Forge Domain)
*   **Date of Death:** April 23, 2026 (Campaign Day 5)
*   **Location:** Oakhaven - The Old Mason's Yard.
*   **Killer:** Void-Touched Sentinel.
*   **Final Act:** Sacrifice. Wael held the line in absolute silence, anchoring Berath's tomb with iron pitons and blessed oil to shield his companions from the void.
*   **Legacy:** Known as the "Smith of the Gap." He mended Oakhaven's spears and forged his own arm (**The Scavenger's Anchor**) and eyepatch (**The Mason's Blinder**) from scrap to stay in the fight.

### Berath (Mountain Dwarf Cleric - Knowledge Domain)
*   **Date of Death:** April 14, 2026 (Campaign Day 1)
*   **Location:** Oakhaven - The Boar and Barrel Tavern.
*   **Killer:** The Pale-Eyed Figure.
*   **Final Act:** Attempted to fuse his soul with the Black Shard. The Shard rejected him, leading to his murder.
*   **Legacy:** Left behind the Dark Black Shard and the journal that began the investigation into the Sorrow.
