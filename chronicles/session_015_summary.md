## Narrative Summary
At the fortified stone hollow of Shepherd's Cache, Skaen and his companions faced the transition from desperate survival to calculated preparation. The session began with the arrival of a Giant Owl, fleeing something unnamed from the Granite Squeeze. The party responded with lethal precision, defending the cache in a swift surprise round that showcased the group's growing tactical cohesion. 

Following the victory, the party committed to an exhaustive period of downtime. Skaen and the Trail-Warden Varin combined their artisan skills to craft a Shortbow from Iron-Bark Yew, while Elora and Alen scoured the local alpine forest for fuel and medicinal reagents. The "White Silence" loomed as a constant pressure, but the party's focus remained unbroken. By the morning of Day 34, the party had successfully brewed a Potion of Healing and fletched twenty arrows, equipping Elora for the descent ahead. The bond between the Dwarf and the Weaver deepened as the tools of survival were shared, turning a collection of refugees into a fortified unit.
