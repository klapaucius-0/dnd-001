## Narrative Summary
The party completed the final leg of the Iron Trek, arriving at the frontier settlement of Oakhaven. The journey was marked by a midnight ambush by Ice Mephits, which the party repelled with coordinated force. Upon entering Oakhaven, Skaen leveraged his artisan skills to restore a seized industrial winch for the scavenger Vesper, earning both gold and a scarred pearl. After establishing a base at the Boar and Barrel, Varin formally committed his bow to Skaen's vision. Skaen utilized his divine magic to restore the pearl's value and identified Alen's cargo as a mundane but essential lead-lined box of reagents, clearing the air of supernatural suspicion before the final delivery to the Granary.
