## Narrative Summary
The party entered the **Shepherd's Bypass**, a rugged timber trail west of Oakhaven. After a day of heavy climbing and successful scavenging, they established a fortified camp at the **High Shelf**. The night passed without incident, but the morning trek was interrupted at the **Granite Squeeze** by a starving **Dire Wolf**. 

Skaen dispatched the beast with a single, searing strike of his hammer. The subsequent investigation of the wolf's lair revealed a discarded scout's satchel containing a map fragment to a **Shepherd's Cache** five miles ahead. The session concluded with Skaen performing an exhaustive harvest of the wolf, securing vital meat, hide, and materials for the journey ahead.
