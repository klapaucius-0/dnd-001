## Narrative Summary
The descent from Shepherd's Cache proved more treacherous than anticipated. Navigating through the deep, deceptive drifts of the Western Reach, the party encountered a hidden fissure in the Granite Squeeze. Skaen fell waist-deep into the snow, causing a significant delay and straining the party's resources.

Upon reaching the narrows, the group was intercepted by two scavengers holding a superior tactical position on a high ledge. A tense standoff ensued, with Skaen attempting to negotiate from behind his shield wall. Refusing to leave hostiles at his back, Skaen led a tactical detour to the High Fissure, circling back above the scavengers. Though the group's stealth was compromised by shifting shale, the ambush was lethal. Elora and Alen neutralized the scavenger archer in a single volley. The remaining thug, cowed by Skaen's presence and the loss of his partner, fled deeper into the Squeeze. 

Refusing to let the act go unrecorded, Skaen utilized his mason's tools and the party's firewood to construct a "Bandit Sentinel"—an impaled warning marker set into the limestone wall, marked with blood-runes. The act deeply unsettled Alen, straining his trust in the dwarf, while Varin viewed it as a pragmatic necessity for maintaining the law of the road. The session ended with the party exiting the Squeeze and entering the open woods of the Western Reach.
