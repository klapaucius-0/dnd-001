# Session 002: The Weeping Shelf

**Date:** Thursday, 16 April 2026
**Location:** The Frozen Gap (The Weeping Shelf -> The Mountain's Throat)
**Characters:** Wael (Cleric 1)

## Narrative Summary
Wael began the day at the Cleft Shelter, integrating the mysterious Black Shard into his blue-green stone focus. Seeking rare materials, he trekked to the Weeping Shelf, but the journey was treacherous, causing minor injuries. On the shelf, Wael encountered Kaelen, a terrified human guide watching the path. Their interaction was interrupted by a Void-Touched Sentinel emerging from a fissure known as the "Mountain's Throat." 

In the ensuing combat, the Sentinel dealt a devastating critical hit, dropping Wael to 0 HP and inflicting a severe internal injury and the loss of his left arm. Wael briefly stabilized and regained consciousness through a surge of willpower (Natural 20 on a death save), but was subsequently struck down again by another critical hit. In a desperate move, Kaelen used a Dark Metal Rod to distract the Sentinel and dragged Wael into the fissure. Sliding into the dark, Wael narrowly survived his final death saving throw by connecting with the resonance of the Black Shard. He is now stable but unconscious in the lower caverns.
