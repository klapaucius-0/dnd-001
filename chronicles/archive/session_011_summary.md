## Narrative Summary
The vigil at the Old Mason's Yard began with the rhythmic scratching of the mountain and ended in the absolute silence of the void. Wael, master-mason and cleric of the Forge, spent his final hours anchoring the rest of his kin, Berath, with iron pitons and blessed oil. When the Yard was besieged by pale, Resonant Figures, Wael held his ground, utilizing the *Mending* cantrip to maintain the structural integrity of the tomb even as blood was spilled around him.

The slab eventually shattered, birthing a Void-Touched Sentinel—a skeletal horror of bone and iron. In the absolute silence emitted by the creature, Wael fought a desperate battle to protect his companions. He succeeded in saving the lives of the guards Toren and Hov, and alongside Kaelen, he battered the Sentinel to the brink of destruction. 

However, the cost was total. Wael fell under the Sentinel's cold-iron claws, his life extinguished in the soundless dark of the Yard. He died as he lived: grounded, unyielding, and protecting those who could not protect themselves.
