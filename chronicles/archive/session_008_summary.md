## Narrative Summary
Wael departed Harlen's Forge and sought immediate employment to test his new prosthetic, the Scavenger's Anchor. At the Oakhaven Granary, he negotiated a high-risk contract with Master Hobb to clear a "rat-dog" infestation in the lower winter rye cellar. The negotiation, bolstered by divine guidance, secured a rare gold crown for the task.

The descent into the cellar turned into a brutal struggle for survival. Three giant rats, driven by an unnatural aggression, nearly claimed Wael's life. After being brought to the brink of death and suffering a permanent injury to his left eye, Wael was stabilized by the frantic and desperate efforts of his companion, Kaelen. Together, they purged the cellar, but at a terrible physical cost. The session ends with Wael and Kaelen, bloodied and broken, resting in the Market Square as they decide where to seek sanctuary for their recovery.
