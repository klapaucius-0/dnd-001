## Narrative Summary
The party completed a grueling 5-mile climb through the Shepherd's Bypass, navigating frozen timber and ice-slicked limestone. Along the way, Skaen and Elora successfully foraged for hemostatic Weaver's Moss and stockpiled fuel from standing dead pines, preparing for a long stay in the heights. Upon reaching the **Shepherd's Cache**, Skaen's dwarven intuition revealed signs of recent, agitated occupancy—the feeding gouges of ghouls etched deep into the stone. With professional precision, Skaen restored a seized padlock and secured vital tools, including woodcarver's implements that would prove essential for the night's work.

Sensing a predatory presence, the party established a "Last Hearth" within the hollow, fortifying the entrance with a resonant bell-wire alarm and jagged pine stakes. The preparation was tested when a pack of three **Ghouls** emerged from the darkness. Skaen anchored the bottleneck, holding a divine field of light while Elora and Alen struck from safety. The victory was absolute, resulting in Skaen's elevation to Level 2. The session concluded with the recruitment of **Varin**, a veteran Trail-Warden, and the completion of several artisan projects that significantly reinforced the party's equipment for the trek ahead.
