# Crafting & Cold-Forming Rules

## 1. Tool Specialization
Crafting in "The Sorrow" is defined by the tools available. A standard smithy is designed for heat; Wael’s craft requires tools that can withstand and exert immense pressure in the cold.

### Tool Tiers
| Tier | Classification | Description | Crafting Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 0 | **Improvised** | Scavenged items, dull hammers, makeshift stones. | Repairs only; Disadvantage on checks. |
| 1 | **Standard Kit** | Standard Smith’s, Mason’s, or Herbalism kits. | Tier 0 materials; Basic maintenance. |
| 2 | **Cold-Forming Implements** | Heavy-duty hammers, precision chisels, and sub-zero cooling agents. | Tier 1 materials; "Cold-Forged" quality. |
| 3 | **Masterwork/Stationary** | High-pressure presses, light-drinking anvils, permanent workshop setup. | Tier 2+ materials; Exceptional/Magical items. |

## 2. The Cold-Forming Mechanic
Cold-forming is the process of "hammering the heat out" and compressing the molecular structure of metal through rhythmic, high-pressure strikes (**Work-Hardening**).

*   **Labor Hours (LH)**: Items have a total LH requirement. 
    *   *Fire-Forging (Reference)*: 1 hour = 5gp of value.
    *   *Cold-Forming*: 1 hour = 1.25gp of value (4x slower).
*   **Strain Hardening (The Benefit)**: Cold-formed items gain the **Cold-Forged** tag.
    *   **Cold-Forged Weapons**: Gain the **Siege** property (deal double damage to objects and structures).
    *   **Cold-Forged Armor**: Grant the wearer **Advantage** on Strength saving throws against being moved or knocked prone.
*   **Strain Fatigue (The Risk)**: Every 4-hour block of work carries a risk of internal stress.
    *   **Success (vs DC)**: Progress made (4 LH).
    *   **Failure**: No progress. The material becomes **Strained** (Disadvantage on the next Artisan Check for this project).
    *   **Failure while Strained**: The material becomes **Brittle**. If used or worked further, it will shatter on a Natural 1.
*   **Cold-Annealing (Stress Relief)**: Wael can spend 4 LH to perform "Stress Relief" (DC 10). A success removes the **Strained** or **Brittle** status. This does not contribute toward the total LH of the project.

## 3. Material Durability & Tool Wear
Materials have a **Hardness Rating (HR)** which determines the DC of the Artisan Check.

| Material | HR (Base DC) | Required Tool Tier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Scrap Wood/Bone | 10 | 0 |
| Standard Steel / Soft Stone | 13 | 1 |
| Hardened Steel / Granite | 15 | 2 |
| Cold-Iron / Deep-Earth Ore | 18+ | 3 |

## 4. Item Maintenance (Repairs)
*   **Minor Repair**: Restoring 1 HP to a damaged object. (1 LH, DC 10).
*   **Structural Restoration**: Fixing a "Broken" item. (4-8 LH, DC 15). Requires 1/4 the original material cost.

## 5. Herbalism & Alchemy
Wael's Herbalism Kit follows similar LH rules but uses Intelligence or Wisdom (Medicine/Nature).
*   **Tinctures/Poultices**: 2 LH per dose.
*   **Complex Decoctions**: 8 LH per dose. Requires specialized glassware.
