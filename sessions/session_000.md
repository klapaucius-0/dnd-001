# Session 00: Character Creation & World Setup

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Status:** Completed

## Character Summary: Berath
*   **Race:** Mountain Dwarf
*   **Class:** Cleric (Knowledge Domain)
*   **Background:** Hermit
*   **Key Stats:** Strength 18 (+4), Wisdom 17 (+3), Charisma 8 (-1).
*   **Core Concept:** A solitary builder drawn to cold, obscure, and sorrowful power.
*   **The Reliquary:** A vibrating leaden box containing a black shard of unknown origin.
*   **Languages:** Common, Dwarvish, Undercommon, Deep Speech, Abyssal.
*   **Skills:** Arcana (Expertise), Religion (Expertise), Insight, Medicine, History, Persuasion.
*   **Starting Equipment:** Battleaxe, Light Crossbow, Scale Mail, Shield (17 AC total), Tinker's Tools, Herbalism Kit, Explorer's Pack.

## Campaign Setup
*   **Setting:** Forgotten Realms, starting in Oakhaven at the Boar and Barrel Tavern.
*   **Tone:** Sad, bleak, gritty, brutal, violent, horror-focused.
*   **Ruleset:** D&D 5e (2014) with Feats, Multiclassing, Variant Human, Flanking, Inspiration, Lingering Injuries, and Morale active.
*   **Sacred Dice:** DM rolls all dice (including for Berath, with permission).
*   **Progression:** XP-based leveling, no plot armor, permanent death.

## The Mystery
*   Berath possesses a unique "Discovery" from his time as a hermit, likely linked to the internal rhythm of the Black Shard.
