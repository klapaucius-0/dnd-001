# World Locations

## Oakhaven
*   **Description:** A hardy frontier town built into the lower slopes of the mountain range. It serves as a vital trade hub for timber and ore, though it feels increasingly isolated as the winter deepens.
*   **Key Spots:**
    *   **The Boar and Barrel Tavern:** A common gathering place for travelers. Known for its thick walls and warm hearth.
    *   **The North Gate:** Heavily guarded entrance. Currently manned by Toren and Hov.

## The Frozen Grove
*   **Description:** A hidden pocket of gnarled, frost-covered trees nestled in a glacier-vent.
*   **Key Spots:**
    *   **Mother Halsa's Shack:** A modest, weathered dwelling where an elderly hermit tends to those who stumble into the grove.

## The Mountain's Throat (Lower Caverns)
*   **Description:** A network of tunnels and fissures beneath the Frozen Gap.
*   **Key Spots:**
    *   **The Ice-Lock:** A narrow passage used by miners, currently under heavy guard or blocked by debris.
    *   **The New Cut:** A relatively fresh mining tunnel leading toward the northern foothills.
    *   **The Western Breach:** A jagged crack in the mountain's side that leads to the surface.

## The Frozen Gap
*   **Description:** A high, narrow mountain pass that marks the boundary between the civilized frontier and the deep wilderness. It is a place of perpetual wind and biting cold.
*   **Key Spots:**
    *   **Cold-Iron Obelisk:** An ancient, weathered ruin at the highest point of the gap. Its dark metal surface is etched with runes that are nearly worn away by the mountain winds. It is known to hum in response to certain minerals or events in the valleys below.
