# World Locations

## Oakhaven
*   **Description:** Frontier town. 
*   **Key Spots:**
    *   **The Boar and Barrel Tavern:** A common gathering place for travelers.

## The Frozen Gap
*   **Description:** A high, narrow mountain pass that marks the boundary between the civilized frontier and the deep wilderness. It is a place of perpetual wind and biting cold.
    *   **Cold-Iron Obelisk:** An ancient, weathered ruin at the highest point of the gap. Its dark metal surface is etched with runes that are nearly worn away by the mountain winds. It is known to hum in response to certain minerals or events in the valleys below.
