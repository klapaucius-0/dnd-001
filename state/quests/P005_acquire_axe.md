# [P005] The Hewer's Need
**Status:** ACTIVE
**Scale:** MINOR
**Related Stratum:** [STRATUM: 1_FRAGILE_SURFACE]

## 1. The Directive (Narrative Goal)
Skaen has identified a critical gap in his survival kit: the lack of a proper axe for processing timber and defense. While his dwarven strength and hammer are sufficient for combat, the efficiency of a sharp edge is required for long-term mountain survival.

## 2. Intelligence & Leads
*   **Lead:** Shepherd's Cache. *Source: Scout's Satchel (Granite Squeeze)* - The cache may contain tools used by mountain-folk.
*   **Lead:** Oakhaven Blacksmith (Harlen). *Source: Alen* - A professional forge is the most reliable place to buy or commission a tool.

## 3. Execution (Sandbox Objectives)
*   **Phase 1:** Acquisition
    *   **Objective A:** [COMPLETED] Scavenge the Shepherd's Cache for discarded or hidden tools.
    *   **Objective B:** [OPEN] Purchase a handaxe or felling axe in Oakhaven (Value: 5 gp for Handaxe / 10 gp for Battleaxe).
    *   **Objective C:** [OPEN] Forge an axe during downtime (Requires Blueprint and Materials).
