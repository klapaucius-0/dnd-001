## Giant Rats [STRATUM: 1_FRAGILE_SURFACE] [NPC_TYPE: STATBLOCK]
**Role/Type:** Small beast, unaligned
**Habitat:** Underground / Urban / Ruin

### Summary
Common vermin that have grown aggressive due to Oakhaven's scarcity.

### Narrative & Identity
*   **Appearance:** Scruffy grey fur; glowing red eyes; needle-sharp teeth.
*   **Sounds:** High-pitched chattering; rapid claw-scratching.
*   **Personality/Behavior:** Scavengers that hunt in swarms.

### Mechanical Details (Unified Entity Protocol)
*   **Base Statblock:** Giant Rat (CR 1/8, MM p. 327)
*   **Modifications:** Narrative "Resonant" theme.

#### Statistics
| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| 7 (-2) | 15 (+2) | 11 (+0) | 2 (-4) | 10 (+0) | 4 (-3) |

#### Combat Block (Strict 5e)
*   **Armor Class:** 12
*   **Hit Points:** 7 (2d6)
*   **Speed:** 30 ft.
*   **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 10
*   **Languages:** ---
*   **Challenge:** 1/8 (25 XP)

### Special Traits
*   **Keen Smell:** The rat has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.
*   **Pack Tactics:** The rat has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the rat's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

### Actions
*   **Bite:** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.

## Dire Wolf [STRATUM: 1_FRAGILE_SURFACE] [NPC_TYPE: STATBLOCK]
**Role/Type:** Large beast, unaligned
**Habitat:** Forest / Arctic / Mountains

### Summary
Massive predatory wolves that hunt in packs. 

### Narrative & Identity
*   **Appearance:** [REVEALED] Pony-sized; thick, frost-matted fur; powerful neck.
*   **Sounds:** Deep, vibrating growls.

### Mechanical Details (Unified Entity Protocol)
*   **Base Statblock:** Dire Wolf (CR 1, MM p. 321)
*   **Modifications:** None (Session 013 specimen was "Starving" - HP 15, DMG 1d10+3).

#### Statistics
| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| 17 (+3) | 15 (+2) | 15 (+2) | 3 (-4) | 12 (+1) | 7 (-2) |

#### Combat Block (Strict 5e)
*   **Armor Class:** 14 (Natural)
*   **Hit Points:** 37 (5d10 + 10)
*   **Speed:** 50 ft.
*   **Skills:** Perception +3, Stealth +4
*   **Senses:** Passive Perception 13
*   **Languages:** ---
*   **Challenge:** 1 (200 XP)

### Special Traits
*   **Keen Hearing and Smell:** The wolf has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.
*   **Pack Tactics:** The wolf has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

### Actions
*   **Bite:** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
