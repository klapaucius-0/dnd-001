## Kaelen [STRATUM: 1_FRAGILE_SURFACE]
A human guide who survived the Frozen Gap. He is currently seeking shelter in Oakhaven.
*   **Appearance:** Wiry and gaunt, wearing salt-stained furs and scavenged leather. His eyes are large and dark with a haunted, "bird-like" quality.
*   **Origin:** The Low-Sallows (south of the Frozen Gap).
*   **Disposition:** Friendly / Indebted.
*   **Relationship:** Level 1 (Trusting).
*   **Personality:** Anxious, observant, and deeply loyal to those who provide safety. He is prone to twitching and quick glances at the shadows.
*   **Outlook on Life:** The world is a predator, and we are all its prey. He believes that survival depends on navigating the fragile social echoes of places like Oakhaven and staying close to "the strong" (like Wael) when the crushing reality of the deeper strata begins to emerge.
*   **Armor Class:** 14 (Observed).
*   **Hit Points:** 16 / 16 (Restored).
*   **Primary Weapon:** Shortbow / Daggers.
*   **Notable Abilities:** Expert Scout (Advantage on Wisdom checks for mountain paths).
*   **Known Facts:**
    *   Born in the Low-Sallows; formerly a mountain guide.
    *   Lost his home and livelihood to the "things in the ice."
    *   Currently possesses nothing but his gear and scavenged clothes.
*   **Inventory:** Shortbow, 2 Daggers, Scavenged clothes, ?? [Unknown].
