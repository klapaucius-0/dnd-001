# Clues & Rumors

## Mysteries & Secrets
*   **The Black Shard:** This is the dark shard of unknown material Wael carries. Berath kept it secret for a reason; it is far more dangerous and valuable than it appears. The stories of people being killed for it are likely true.
*   **Berath's Journal (The Great Ruse):** It is now a confirmed fact that the "Still-Forge" and the "Deep Frost Network" mentioned in the journal are **misinformation**. Berath used these myths to mislead anyone who might seize his notes. His true ambitions were humble and focused on building and crafting.
*   **The Cold-Iron Obelisk:** A profound mystery. While it reacts to the mountain's cold, its true purpose and the identity of its creators remain unknown. This truth will only be revealed much later in Wael's journey.


## Known Leads
*   **Pale-Eyed Figures:** Silent, frost-filled constructs or beings that haunt the high passes.
*   **Dark Metal Rods:** Foci for freezing energy, seemingly linked to the Cold-Iron Obelisk.
