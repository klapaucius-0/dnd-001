# Clues & Rumors

## Mysteries & Secrets
*   **The Dark Black Shard:** This is the dark shard of unknown material Wael carries. Berath was murdered for it. It is exceptionally heavy and seems to draw the attention of the Pale-Eyed Figures.
*   **Berath's Journal (The Great Ruse) - [CONCLUDED]:** The "Still-Forge" and the "Deep Frost Network" mentioned in the journal were confirmed to be **misinformation**. Berath used these myths to protect his notes. His true ambitions were humble and focused on building and crafting.
*   **The Cold-Iron Obelisk:** A weathered ruin in the Gap. Its true purpose remains unknown.
*   **The Mountain's Throat - [RESOLVED]:** A fissure at the Weeping Shelf leading to lower caverns. It is a place where the "stillness" of the sentinels seems to falter.
*   **The Still-Eyes (Network) - [CONCLUDED]:** Initial fears of a vast network were likely the result of mountain-madness or the shard's own influence. The Pale-Eyed Figures are solitary monsters, not a coordinated force.
*   **The Hum - [DEBUNKED]:** The "frequency" Kaelen spoke of was likely a hallucination brought on by extreme cold and terror.

## Known Leads
*   **Oakhaven:** Berath was murdered at the Boar and Barrel three nights ago. A local witness described a hooded figure in a black cloak who moved with unnatural speed and "wrongness." The killer used a dark blade to stab Berath after a brief, silent struggle. Berath's body was taken to the **Old Mason's Yard**.
