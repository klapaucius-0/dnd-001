# The Graveyard of Berath

This document records the fallen souls who have walked this bleak frontier and the circumstances of their final, cold moments.

## Fallen Heroes

### Berath (Mountain Dwarf Cleric)
*   **Date of Death:** April 14, 2026
*   **Location:** Oakhaven - The Boar and Barrel Tavern.
*   **Killer:** The Pale-Eyed Figure.
*   **Final Act:** Attempted to fuse his soul with the Black Shard. The Shard rejected him.
*   **Legacy:** The Black Shard and the Reliquary were claimed by the killer.
