# Artifacts of the Deep-Form
<!-- SOURCE: artifacts -->

This file contains high-resolution physical data for unique items. These descriptions represent the "objective reality" Wael perceives through his artisan's intuition.

---

## 1. The Damp Blue-Green Stone Box
**Current Status:** Mounted as a spellcasting focus in the center of Wael's damaged shield. It contains the Dark Black Shard.

### Physical Properties
*   **Dimensions:** Roughly 3" x 1.5" x 1". The interior cavity is sized to hold the Shard with significant extra room.
*   **Weight:** 2.0 lbs.
*   **Composition:** An unidentifiable, stone-like mineral with the color of oxidized copper and deep-earth silt. It is harder than granite; Wael's current Tier 1 tools cannot mark its surface.
*   **Condition:** Ancient. The edges are slightly rounded by what appears to be aeons of tectonic pressure rather than water erosion.

### Sensory Profile
*   **Touch:** Perpetually "damp" or clammy, like stone deep within a lightless cave, yet it leaves no moisture on the hand.
*   **Sight:** The color shifts slightly between a bruised blue and a sickly, pale green depending on the angle of the light.
*   **Effect:** It possesses an unyielding "stillness." Its primary function appears to be as an anchor or dampener, physically calming the erratic energy of the item held within.

## 2. The Dark Black Shard
**Current Status:** Secured inside the Damp Blue-Green Stone Box.

### Physical Properties
*   **Dimensions:** Roughly the size of a dwarf’s thumb, jagged and irregular.
*   **Weight:** Impossibly dense. It weighs exactly 5.0 lbs despite its small size.
*   **Composition:** A matte, non-reflective material. It does not possess a grain, crystalline structure, or metallic sheen. It appears to "absorb" the light around its edges.
*   **Frequency:** It vibrates at a low, nearly imperceptible frequency. This vibration is felt as a bone-deep "hum" rather than a sound, which is noticeably suppressed when the Box is closed.
*   **Temperature:** Persistently cold, regardless of ambient environment. It never "warms up" to Wael's touch.

### Sensory Profile
*   **Touch:** Unnaturally smooth, yet it provides immense friction. It feels as though it is "pulling" toward the center of the earth.
