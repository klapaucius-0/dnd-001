# Campaign Bestiary

This file contains stat blocks and tactical notes for creatures encountered during the campaign.

## Monsters of the Gap

### Pale-Eyed Figure
*Small/Medium humanoid, chaotic neutral*
*   **Armor Class:** 12 (Estimated)
*   **Hit Points:** 15 (Estimated)
*   **Speed:** 30 ft.
*   **Attacks:** 
    *   **Cold-Chilled Hands:** +3 to hit (Estimated). 1d6 cold damage.
*   **Tactics:** Uses terrain to ambush and strike quickly before retreating.
*   **Notes:** One was killed by Wael near the Obelisk. They seem attracted to the Dark Black Shard.

### Pale-Eyed Sentinel
*Large skeletal construct, neutral evil*
*   **Armor Class:** 14
*   **Hit Points:** 30
*   **Speed:** 30 ft.
*   **Attacks:** 
    *   **Multiattack:** The sentinel makes two Frost Claw attacks.
    *   **Frost Claw:** +4 to hit. 2d4+2 cold/slashing damage.
*   **Special Abilities:**
    *   **Lethal Precision:** On a critical hit with a Frost Claw, the target must sustain a **Lingering Injury** (roll on DMG table).
    *   **Cold Aura (Passive):** Emits an unnatural cold that can freeze wounds shut, preventing immediate bleeding but causing necrotic bruising.
*   **Tactics:** Brutal, relentless melee combatants. They focus on the source of the Dark Black Shard's resonance and do not tire.
*   **Notes:** One nearly killed Wael on the Weeping Shelf. Another was seen frozen into the ice curtain at the Western Breach, acting as a "sentinel-gate."
